Andrea Moro's search plugins

JSTOR search right at your fingertips! Mozilla Firefox's toolbar contains a search box (next  to the url address box). By default, the search box performs a Google search, but you can select other search engines by clicking on the small arrow next to Google's "G" icon. You can add Jstor among the list of engines by clicking on the "Install" links below. You might need to restart Firefox. Check also the FAQs below.

Many thanks to Erik Marsh from UCSB for sending me a nice Jstor icon.

FAQ

How do I uninstall this or other search engines?

There is a specific firefox plugin that does this. You can google for it, or simply you can go to your Firefox installation directory (typically: "C:/Program Files/Mozilla Firefox"), and open the subdirectory called "searchplugins"; delete the two files with filenames corresponding to the engine you want to remove. Then, restart Firefox
